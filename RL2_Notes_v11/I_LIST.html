Software titles protected with RapidLok2


The following games are known to be protected with RapidLok2.

This list may not be complete. Keep in mind that even if a title is
listed below this does not necessarily mean all versions of it are
protected with RapidLok2: There may exist versions with a different
RapidLok version, a non-RapidLok protection or even no protection
at all.

Game                   Company      TV type
--------------------------------------------
AcroJet                Microprose        PAL
Conflict in Vietnam    Microprose   NTSC
Destiny                Destiny      NTSC
Law of the West        Accolade          PAL
Psi5 Trading Company   Accolade          PAL

Notes:
"Law of the West" NTSC is RapidLok1 protected.
There are "Psi5 Trading Company" versions with RL1 and RL5 protection.
