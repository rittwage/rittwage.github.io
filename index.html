Home/Welcome
These pages represent a collection of my interests, as well as past and present projects. I continue to develop several small games and tools that I always wanted to have and use myself. Possibly they'll interest others, too.

I grew up in the 8-bit computing era during the reign of the Commodore 64. I've made small contributions to the emulation community since my first Centipede emulator (VPCB) released in late 1996. This was before MAME existed and Dave Spicer had just stunned everyone with the first arcade game emulator, DASARCADE (which later evolved into SPARCADE).
